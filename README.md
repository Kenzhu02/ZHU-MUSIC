- Program Updated

## Commands 🛠

- `/play <song name>` - play song you requested
- `/song <song name>` - download songs you want quickly
- `/hexor` - bot online or offline

#### Admins Only 👷‍♂️
- `/pause` - pause song play
- `/resume` - resume song play
- `/skip` - play next song
- `/end` - stop music play

#### Sudo User Onlu
- `/gcast` - Boadcast Your Massage
